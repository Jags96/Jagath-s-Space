### 2D representations

- $ **x** $ represents column vector 
- $**/{x^~}**$ represents 

$$
\vec{x} = 
\begin{bmatrix}
a \\
b
\end{bmatrix}
$$

This is inline math: $x + y = z$

This is display math:

$$
\vec{x} = \begin{bmatrix} a \\ b \end{bmatrix}
$$

$$
\vec{x} =
\begin{pmatrix}
a \\ \\
b \not{b}
\vdots 
\end{pmatrix}
$$
